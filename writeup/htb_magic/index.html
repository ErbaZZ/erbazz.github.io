Let me show you a Magic! The is a Medium difficulty Linux box that employs old but still relevant tricks. You need to know the Magic and how Linux operates with files to clear this box.

Difficulty: Medium
Points: 30
Release: 18 Apr 2020
IP: 10.10.10.185

This Writeup is Encrypted

Unlocking

Windows Machine: Use the Administrator SAM hash (copy from Administrator to :::)
